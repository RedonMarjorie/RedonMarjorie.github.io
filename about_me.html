About me

Marjorie Redon, M.Sc.

Brief Summary

I am a Phisician Engineer from l'Université du Mans. I obtained my Master's Degree on Phtonics Signal Imagering at the Université d'Angers. I've worked as a work-student at the ImHorPhen research group, led by David Rousseau. Currently, I am a PhD student at Unicaen under the supervision of Abderrahim Elmoataz and Yvain Quéau.

A little bit about my life

I lived a long part my life in St Christophe du Luat, a small village in France. France is known for its culture, its rich culinary heritage , and it's history. During my studies I left my small vilage for Le Mans then Angers, a beautiful green city where life is good.

I decided to study Physics Engineering as it was a mix between optics and signal processing. Half-way through my undergrad studies, I felt in love with machine learning algorithms and research. I decided to continue along this path and, in my master's studies, I worked with David Rousseau where I enhanced my research skills.
